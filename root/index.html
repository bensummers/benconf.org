About BenConf

BenConf Benefactor

Ben Chestnut, co-founder and CEO of MailChimp, is the
Official BenConf Benefactor!

Ben and the lovely lovely people at MailChimp are handing over a big pile of cash, and the only thing they want in return is, and I quote, "make it special for my fellow Bens".

So, thanks to his appreciation of fine Ben-related jokes, there will be pudding, drinks and swag at BenConf.

Thanks Ben!
