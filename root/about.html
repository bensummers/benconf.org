About BenConf

A serious technical event

BenConf grew out of a a list of UK technical people on twitter called Ben, and a not entirely serious blog post about organising a conference for technical people called Ben.

Surprisingly, there are enough silly people in the world that the blog post got read by tens of thousands of people, and the evening sold out within a couple of weeks.

Even more surprisingly, Ben Chestnut, founder of MailChimp, thought it would be a good idea to sponsor the evening.

Although it started as a joke, it's a Serious Technical Event. There are lots of events which concentrate on a single technology or industry, and it's hard to find somewhere you can talk about lots of different technical things. And you get hungry.

BenConf is the event you've been waiting for: Lots of interesting technical people talking about lots of different technical things, and a proper dinner at the right time.
